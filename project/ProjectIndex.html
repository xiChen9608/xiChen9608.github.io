Research on Crowd Abnormal Behavior Detection Based on Social Force Model

a research method based on social force model to detect crowd abnormal behavior is proposed in this paper. At first, optical flow with Lucas-Kanade method is used to calculate the optical flow field of the feature points, and then the social force model is applied to simulate crowd behavior movement by calculating the interaction forces of the feature points. And then the extracted features are converted into visual words by the method of bag of words. Finally, the support vector machine is utilized to detect abnormal events after being trained using the feature.
